A Shared Mission

We aim to enrich the Santa Fe community by cultivating shared creative spaces that amplify underrepresented voices while fostering equity, unity, and belonging.

This vision stems from extensive community engagement and collaboration spanning five years, where local residents and artists have contributed ideas, hopes, and recommendations that underpin our ambitions.
