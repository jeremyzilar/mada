The Midtown Arts District is a creative nexus that will serve as an extension of our collective stories, reaching from the historic Plaza and Canyon Road to newcomer communities across the city. It will encapsulate the full breadth of creativity, talent, and innovation present in Santa Fe.

MADA (Midtown Art District Alliance) is dedicated to expanding upon Santa Fe's rich histories and diverse cultural narratives, solidifying its status as a globally-recognized creative center and protected heritage site of cultural convergence.
