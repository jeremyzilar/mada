Our Impact Goals

Our mission and goals stem from extensive community engagement and collaboration spanning five years, where local residents and artists have contributed ideas, hopes, and recommendations that underpin our ambitions. Reach out if you want to get involved or have ideas on ways to measure positive impact.

01

Cultural Goals

To bring together Santa Fe’s north and south sides through a creative nexus

02

Economic Goal

To bridge creative practice with education and career pathways for people of all ages

03

Community Goal

To connect Northern New Mexico with artists and creative practitioners globally

04

City Goal

To ensure Santa Fe’s relevance as a cultural and creative mecca for generations to come
