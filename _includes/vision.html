MADA is repurposing 50,000 sq ft of abandoned buildings on the Santa Fe Midtown Campus, including the iconic Visual Arts Center buildings, designed by Ricardo Legorreta, and the last remaining Barracks from the historic WWII Brunns Military Hospital.

We have reached over 30% of our fundraising goals to date! In summer 2025, we will execute a long term ground lease with the City and hope to break ground in early 2026! Transformative projects like this are only possible with the enthusiasm and support of our community partners!
