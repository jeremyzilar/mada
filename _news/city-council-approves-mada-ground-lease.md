The Santa Fe City Council unanimously approved the development agreement and ground lease with the Midtown Arts and Design Alliance on Wednesday — a milestone years in the making. The decision clears the way for MADA to begin transforming the Midtown campus into a community hub for arts, culture, and creative industry.
