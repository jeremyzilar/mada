What is MADA?

The Midtown Arts and Design Alliance (MADA) is a creative cultural center in the center of Santa Fe that will provide affordable space and accessible arts, design, and cultural education and programming for community members of all ages.

Current Alliance Members

+

CENTER Santa Fe

CENTER Santa Fe is a non-profit that supports socially and environmentally engaged lens-based projects through education, public platforms, and partnerships.

+

Creative Startups

Creative Startups provides support and resources to help creative entrepreneurs and startups thrive, which it achieves through specialized training, mentorship, and access to a network of experts and investors.

+

Currents New Media

Currents New Media fosters new media arts and presents artists creating innovative work. Their annual festival, educational programs, and exhibitions provide year-round public access to immersive and expansive art experiences.

+

Girls, Inc

Girls, Inc. offers long-lasting mentoring relationships, a pro-girl environment, and research-based programming to equip youth to navigate gender, economic, and social barriers and grow up healthy, educated, and independent.

+

Little Globe

Little Globe partners with local, national, and international communities to create rigorous artistic works that create meaningful relationships, empower individuals and communities, and reflect the power of our social imagination. 'Connect, Heal, and Thrive through the art of Storytelling.'

+

MAKE Santa Fe

MAKE Santa Fe is a non-profit community workspace dedicated to catalyzing the creative class, where people can access tools, resources, and workshops to make, repair, invent, or create anything they can dream of.

+

MASS Design Group

MASS Design Group’s mission is to research, build, and advocate for architecture that promotes justice and human dignity.

+

Santa Fe Art Institute

Santa Fe Art Institute forges critical inquiry and cultural exchange among artists, creative practitioners, and the broader community. SFAI supports and amplifies dynamic artistic practices that engage complex social issues, inspire individual transformation, and inform collective action.

+

Santa Fe Indigenous Center

Santa Fe Indigenous Center is designed by and for Indigenous Peoples with the mission of supporting, promoting, and enriching their vital, diverse community by identifying and serving the needs and interests of their people.

+

The University of New Mexico

The University of New Mexico and its partnering divisions (College of Fine Arts, Continuing Education, School of Architecture + Planning, and STEAM NM) serve as the State’s flagship institution of higher learning through demonstrating and growing excellence in teaching, research, and service.

+

Vital Spaces

Vital Spaces sustains and enhances Santa Fe’s cultural vibrancy by creating affordable spaces for artists working in all media. They focus on fostering a collaborative creative community and supporting people, ideas, and art forms that are underrepresented in Santa Fe’s commercial art scene.

+

YouthWorks

YouthWorks! is a cutting-edge, innovative organization offering a continuum of services designed to reconnect 'at risk' and disadvantaged youth ages 14-25 with their community through education, employment training, and job placement.

A Shared Mission

We aim to enrich the Santa Fe community by providing shared, creative spaces that amplify underrepresented voices and foster equity, unity, and belonging.

Our vision incorporates extensive community engagement and collaboration spanning five years, with contributions from local residents and artists about their ideas, hopes, and dreams about the future of Midtown.

Our Impact Goals

When complete, MADA will serve as a new arts center that expands the story we can tell about art, culture, and creativity in our city. Reach out if you want to get involved or have ideas on ways to measure positive impact.

01

Cultural

To bring together Santa Fe’s north and south sides through a creative nexus

02

Economic

To bridge creative practice with education and career pathways for people of all ages

03

Community

To provide accessible, all-ages programming that celebrates our multicultural community

04

City

To ensure Santa Fe’s relevance as a cultural and creative mecca for generations to come

Our Focus

+

Community Connections

To create the first creative hub of its kind for Santa Fe, bridging the north and south sides.

+

Affordable Space & Programs

To offer affordable arts and culture programs to 30,000+ residents and visitors annually, including studios, galleries, maker spaces, performance sites, event venues, and communal areas that welcome everyone.

+

Art as Social Change

To invest in art, design, and advocacy as a force for justice and equity.

+

Workforce Development

To combine creative practice with education, impactful social services, apprenticeship and career pathways, and connect New Mexico with creative practitioners from around the world.

MADA

The Midtown Arts and Design Alliance (MADA) is a creative nexus that will serve as an extension of our collective stories, as a place of belonging and potential for emerging and excluded voices.

Building upon Santa Fe’s status as a globally recognized site of cultural convergence, MADA is dedicated to expanding the full breadth of creativity, talent, and innovation present in Santa Fe through creative cultural exchange.
